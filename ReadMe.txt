************
* Controls *
************

Player:
'W' or VK_UP - Move player up
'S' or VK_DOWN - Move player down
'A' or VK_LEFT - Turn player left
'D' or VK_RIGHT - Turn player right
VK_SPACE - Shoot normal bullets form player
'H' - Shoot homing bullets from player
'O' - Shoot black hole from player
'P' - Shoot white hole from player

Miscellaneous:
VK_ESCAPE - Exit program
VK_RETURN - Go from main menu screen to game screen or from win-lose screen to main menu screen
VK_UP and VK_DOWN - Toggle between available levels for selection in main menu screen
